"TramOramic Tour"

Hong Kong is well-known as the Pearl of the Orient because of its amazing night scenery. To fully enjoy it, you can book a "TramOramic Tour", a sightseeing tour on tram organizes by the Hong Kong Tramways. You will be given a guided tour on the authentic tales of local life and tram history. What is great is that it is available in 8 languages! During the tour, you can always hop on and hop off as the tram goes, making your trip highly flexible.

Harbour City

Harbour City is a shopping mall located in the heart of the city -- Tsim Sha Tsui. As it is built right next to the Victoria Harbour, you can always enjoy the harbour view when you dine-in in most of the restaurants in the shopping mall. To enjoy the harbour view fully, I would recommend you to go up to the top level of the mall's parking building. Here you can have a 360-degree harbour view. The view is stunning when the sun sets.

Cityplaza

CityPlaza is a shopping mall located in the east of Hong Kong Island. It is easily accessible by MTR. The highlight of the shopping mall is that there is a ice rank called Cityplaza Ice Palace! Rent a pair of boots to start ice skating or attend a trial class offered by the Ice Palace!

Hong Kong Disneyland

Disneyland is always a dream place for parents and kids. Hong Kong Disneyland is very close to the Hog Kong International Airport. You can travel by the MTR and it takes only around 45 mins to the park. If you are a Disney fan, don't miss the chance to purchase Hong Kong limited edition products of your favorite Disney characters! (We have special editions for different local festivals here!)

Ocean Park Hong Kong

Ocean Park is one of the biggest wildlife conservation organizations in Hong Kong. Come with your kids and learn about wildlife conservation and bio-diversity here. As a SAR of China, we have pandas in the park too. Grab the chance to meet these lovely pandas!
