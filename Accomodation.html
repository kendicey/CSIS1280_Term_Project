The Peninsula

The Peninsula hotel is propably the oldest hotel in Hong Kong. Operating since 1928, the Hong Kong Peninsula hotel is well-known for its superior service and its location just next to Victoria Harbour. Residents can view the beautiful night city scenery as well as new year fireworks while sitting comfortly in their luxury hotel rooms.

Rosewood Hong Kong

The Rosewood Hong Kong is a young luxury hotel brand in Hong Kong. Located in East Tsim Sha Tsui, residents can also enjoy the full harbour view in their rooms. The Rosewood Hong Kong is popular among young people because of its extraordinary artistic hotel room design. Together with the harbour view, many young people spend their nights there for taking attracting instagram-able photos.

Holiday Inn

Holiday Inn is an international hotel brand that offers middle-ranged price accomodations. Their are many advantages of having Holiday Inn as your travel accomodation. Below are some of them:

  1. Reasonable price with good service
  2. Reliable as it is an international coperation
  3. Though rooms are not as fancy as the luxury hotels', they are highly practical with all essentials you would need
  4. Many branches available even in the same cities so you can choose the one suits your itenery most

YHA Mei Ho House Youth Hostel

YHA Mei Ho House Youth Hostel is also known as the Mei Ho House. Located in the heart of Kowloon, tourist attractions are mostly less than an hour travel. The YHA Mei Ho House Youth Hostel is part of the Mei Ho House Revitalisation Project, to revitalise the 1954 built Mei Ho House, turning a heritage to a youth hostel. There is a permanent exhibition located in the hostel's ground floor to record the history and let tourist learn more about the nearby community.

Apart from standard rooms, as a youth hostel, Mei Ho House also provides 4-Person Dormitory Bed and 8-Person Dormitory Bed to travellers with limited budget. These bed types can cost as low as CA$45 so it helps young travellers save a lot.
