Dim Sum

Eating Dim Sum is one of a must-do items for many Hong Kong families. Usually in weekend morning, parents will bring their kids to go "yum cha" to meet grandparents.

Lin Heung Kui (蓮香居) is a very famous Dim Sum restaurant in Hong Kong. It has been in operation for more than a century. Lin Heung Kui serves high quality and authentic traditional Dim Sum. It is also recognized by MICHELIN guide for years. Due to its class and popularity, public figures like celebrities and politicians can be easily seen in the restaurant.

Sushi

Hong Kong people's love on Japanese food and culture has bring Hong Kong many delicious and reasonably-prized Japanese restaurants.

Sushiro (壽司郎) is the most popular Japanese chain resturants in Hong Kong. Although there are nearly 20 branches open, you can always see a super long line waiting to dine-in. The reason why Sushiro is so popular is that it offers a wide variety of food choice with quality and fresh ingredients. The restaurant's menu changes every 2-3 months so you will never get bored.

The Korean Street

If you are a fan of korean TV dramas, Tsim Sha Tsui is one of the places that you would like to go when you visit Hong Kong.

Kimberley Street in Tsim Sha Tsui is known by local people as the "Korean Street". There are many Korean restuarants operated by Koreans in that area so it is the place where you can find authentic Korean food. Apart from that, there is also a Korean supermarket called the New World Mart where you can find all sorts of korean groceries and kimchi.

Authentic Thai

As an international city, there are many foreigners in Hong Kong. Kowloon City is a district that many thai people lives in. Local people recognize the district as the "little Thailand". The Thai community in Kowloon City opens many Thai restaurants in the district, from BBQ skewer to Tom Yum soup, you can always find the best authentic Thai food in the area.

PHAYA THAI is one of the best Thai restaurants I have ever tried in Kowloon City. I highly recommend their prawn pad-thai and soft-shell crabs with pumpkin curry. They are both heavenly delicious!
